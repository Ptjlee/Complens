## 2. Video Script: "The Boardroom Fire Drill & The 5% Trap"

**Format Guidelines:**
*   **Length:** 60-90 seconds
*   **Vibe:** Professional but raw. Lean forward, give inside advice to a peer over a coffee.
*   **Setup:** Well-lit, looking directly into the lens. No green screens, use a modern room/office.

**(0:00 - 0:08) The Hook: Validate their day-to-day pain.**
"If you are managing HR data at a mid-to-large company right now, I know exactly what your screen looks like. It’s a mess of fragmented system exports, broken VLOOKUPs, and endless spreadsheets."

**(0:08 - 0:28) The Authority & The Executive Request:**
"I’m Peter. I've spent my career leading HR strategy at an executive level, most recently heading HR for Europe at Hyundai. And if there is one thing I learned managing headcount at that scale, it’s this: no matter how expensive your enterprise HR software is, pulling reliable data is an absolute nightmare. Every time the CEO or CFO asked for a sudden headcount or compensation report, it triggered a massive internal fire drill. We were wasting entire days locked in spreadsheets, just doing manual math."

**(0:28 - 0:55) The Escalation (To Compliance):**
"But the day-to-day reporting is just the start. With the new EU Pay Transparency Directive, HR’s worst nightmare is now a legal reality: the employee's *'Right to Know.'* Any employee can suddenly demand their pay parity data. And if your overall gap hits 5%, you fall into the *'5% Trap,'* where you are legally on the clock. The truth is, you don’t always have to blindly raise salaries—the EU allows you to explain pay gaps with objective criteria and a six-month action plan. But trying to manually track those individual justifications and remediation plans in Excel? It's a disaster waiting to happen. If you fail to justify or fix those gaps in time, you trigger a massive Joint Pay Assessment, and the lawsuits and fines kick in."

**(0:55 - 1:15) The Pivot & The Solution:**
"I got so frustrated with this industry standard that I built the exact tool I wished I had. CompLens was designed by HR, for HR. We take your messy data exactly as it exports from your system. Within minutes, it gives you an audit-proof dashboard that instantly identifies justifiable gaps and automatically tracks your compliance action plans. It compresses months of manual work into a few clicks—so you can answer any boardroom or employee request with total confidence."

**(0:70 - End) The Call to Action:**
"Sign up below to try CompLens yourself, or book a quick demo with me. I'd love to show you how it works."
